FAQ

How long until I receive my order ?

Packaging of your order is done within the same day for orders before 6pm PST and is sent off to the carrier where delivery times vary. You can track your order via the confirmation number you received after you placed the order.

How do returns work?

Orders within 14 days of purchase are eligible for a refund. There is a $4 base reshelving fee. Refund amount depends on the condition of the book when received by our distribution centers.
